8 Vegetables You Must Grow In Monsoons

Monsoon season is upon us and dewy wind will soon make our ambience even more serene.

Smell of wet soil, breezy evenings and nights adorned with mist is every old school's dream and monsoon gives us exactly that. But do you know this is the perfect time to get started with vegetable gardening?

In India, the monsoon season usually lasts for four months starting from June to September and makes for an excellent time to grow any plant.

As there is a drop in temperature, the environment and moisture aids to the growth of plants and many vegetables are suitable to grow in this weather.

So, if you are looking for a starter information about growing fresh vegetables at your home, here are a few vegetables you can pick for your monsoon ready kitchen garden.

Sweet Potato, Shakarkand (Ipomoea batatas), sweet potatoes are best to be grown in monsoon season. These are super easy to grow in large containers or backyards. This unique vegetable is mostly boiled or baked and then used in traditional dishes such as chaats and salads.
