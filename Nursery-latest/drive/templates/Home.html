Nursery is an Indian online platform that sells plants, gardening supplies, and related products.

It was founded in 2014 with the aim of making gardening accessible to everyone. Nursery offers a variety of plants, tools, fertilizers, and other gardening accessories.

The platform also provides resources such as articles, videos, and a gardening community for enthusiasts to share their knowledge and experiences.
